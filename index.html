About Me

I am a PhD candidate in CS at NTU, focusing on generative models. With a strong foundation in applied mathematics and expertise in mathematical modeling, I develop AI algorithms that combine theoretical rigor with practical impact, with research interests spanning diffusion models, physics AI, and representation learning. I am also committed to human-centered AI and strive to build technologies that are both technically grounded and meaningful to people. With a mindset rooted in design thinking and cross-domain problem solving, I enjoy working at the intersection of research, creativity, and real-world applications. I’m passionate about impactful innovation and open to collaborations in AI and creative technology.
